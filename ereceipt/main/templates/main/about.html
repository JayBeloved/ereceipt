What is C.A.R.E.S ?

The Collaborative Academic Resource Exchange System (CARES), is an of the Redeemer's University Accounting Student Association (RUNASA) executives and the Academic Committee of the department!

Why C.A.R.E.S ?

This initiative aims to create a centralized repository of essential course materials and past questions, ensuring that all students have easy access to the resources they need to excel academically. By contributing your materials, you're not only helping fellow students but also fostering a collaborative learning environment within the department.

E-Receipt

E-Receipt is our digital solution to simplify payment tracking and record-keeping for students in the department. Our system enables quick and secure access to electronic receipts for verified transactions, providing a convenient and eco-friendly way to manage payments.
